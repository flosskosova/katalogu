# License criteria for this catalog

## Included

- **OSI-approved licenses** (e.g. MIT, Apache-2.0, GPL family, MPL, BSD, ISC, LGPL where applicable).
- Other **widely recognized free software licenses** that meet the same spirit (copyleft or permissive) when they are clearly used in a FOSS-compatible way.

## Excluded from recommendations

- **Source-available** models that reserve essential freedoms (e.g. use, modification, redistribution under the same terms) in ways that do not qualify as open source.
- **License changes** that moved formerly open projects behind restrictive terms; such products are dropped or replaced with community forks where those forks are mature.

When in doubt, the catalog prefers projects whose **current** default distribution is under an OSI-approved license.
